Our Mission at Aircloak

is to transform user analytics through next-generation user privacy technology.

User analytics, whether for business, health, or government, is hampered by inadequate protections for user privacy. It is risky and costly to share user data between organizations, or often even between divisions of large multi-nationals or government branches. Using sensitive user data, like location, can involve a lengthy government approval process as well as 1st-generation anonymization that weakens the value of the data.

Organizations are missing valuable user data that could otherwise be used to improve health, streamline government operations, reduce fraud, or enable better marketing insights.
At Aircloak, we are building the next generation of private-by-design analytics technology. If you are a health, government, or business organization frustrated by the cost and risk of user data collection and analytics, read our white paper, or come talk to us and see how better privacy enables better analytics.
