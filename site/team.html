Founders

Felix Bauer graduated from the University of Cambridge with a major in Physics. On the side, he spent his time managing the family business and student organizations, and taking jobs in innovation management and as a freelance scientific consultant.

At Aircloak, he's combining his technical and business background to help create a company with real positive impact using extraordinary technology and ideals.
Prof. Dr. Paul Francis first came up with the idea for Aircloak. He has over 20 years experience in academic and industrial computer science research and is currently a Director at the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems. There, he has concentrated on privacy in analytics and advertising.

Now, he's making sure Aircloak offers bullet-proof privacy and a level of transparency never seen before.
Sebastian Probst Eide graduated with a major in Computer Science from the University of Cambridge. Before that, he had the opportunity to collect years of programming experience, for example when working for the back then highly confidential Google+ team.

As technical lead and a true visionary, he's constantly expanding Aircloak's functionality and pushes the boundaries of what is possible.
