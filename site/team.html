The Aircloak Team


Aircloak is a small, focused team. We keep politics to a minimum, so our vision and our customers can stay top priorities. We work completely distributed – that way, we can get the best people for the job, from anywhere in the world.



Felix Bauer

graduated from the University of Cambridge with a major in Physics. On the side, he spent his time managing the family business and student organizations, and taking jobs in innovation management and as a freelance scientific consultant.
At Aircloak, he's combining his technical and business background to help create a company with extraordinary technology and ideals.

Sebastian Probst Eide

graduated with a major in Computer Science from the University of Cambridge. He had the opportunity to collect years of programming experience, for example when working for the Google+ team.
As technical lead and a true visionary, he's constantly expanding Aircloak's functionality and pushes the boundaries of what is possible.

Prof. Paul Francis

has over 20 years experience in academic and industrial computer science research and is currently a Director at the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems.
Now, he's making sure Aircloak offers bullet-proof privacy and a level of transparency never seen before.

Cristian Berneanu

spent seven years protecting computer users from malware and hackers before joining Aircloak.
Now, he goes over every little detail of the Aircloak system to ensure customers' data is locked down as tight as possible.

Matthias Kretschmer

has seven years of experience working in a theory group at the computer science department of the university of Bonn. He now uses his background in theoretical computer science to the advantage of Aircloak and helps to build a sound anonymity enforcing system.

Sasa Juric

is an all-round versatile programmer with more than 10 years of professional experience in development of large server systems as well as desktop applications. He is a self-appointed Erlang/Elixir evangelist on his blog The Erlangelist, and is in the process of writing the upcoming book Elixir in Action.
