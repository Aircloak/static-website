We are looking for a remote software engineer
interested in tackling daunting challenges!

At Aircloak we develop a query and anonymisation layer that our customers put in front of their highly sensitive personal data. Using our technology they avoid accidentally leaking sensitive data, and gain the ability to exploratively work with, and share their data with untrusted third parties – effectively opening up untapped data in a completely safe way.

The challenges in building a system like ours are countless. At the core of it lies our ability to provide a rich query interface, while ensuring that no query can ever leak sensitive information about individuals. Our technology stems out of years of research at the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems; We have academic backgrounds from the University of Cambridge, Bell Labs and Cornell University. But it takes a killer team to make a strong technology like ours a commercial success – and that’s where you come into play.
Elixir and Erlang are the cornerstones of our architecture. They allow us to quickly build reliable systems that can respond to queries in soft real-time. We deploy our product with docker.

We are ...

a strong team – including the author of one of the best selling books on Elixir – and are looking to hire additional full stack software engineers.

We work ...

remotely, and the team is spread all over Europe. You should be willing to work remotely too, and should be situated within two time-zones of Berlin. We organise periodic retreats in beautiful locations to fuse together as a team. Our work style relies on the fact that you are self-driven, can work independently, and possess stellar communication skills.

We offer ...

competitive salaries, equity, and an exciting and friendly work environment.

Skills & Requirements:
Your background matters less than your clear head, problem solving skills, critical reasoning abilities, and ability to reduce problems to their bare essential. We are of the opinion that anyone who is smart and dedicated enough can quickly pick up the tools and techniques needed. Therefore, worry not if you don't yet know Elixir. What we like to see in our candidates is proven industry experience, or some comparable track record of excellence.

We offer competitive salaries, equity, sensible working hours and an exciting and friendly work environment. We believe you get more and better work done when you have a healthy work life balance, and actively encourage that.
