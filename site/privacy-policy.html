Our privacy policy in short


aircloak.com collects only the user information needed to run the specific services it provides (for instance mailing lists or customer accounts), and nothing more. aircloak.com never shares user information except that needed to run its services, or when legally required to do so. Aircloak will inform its users if this policy changes.


Comprehensive version


aircloak.com is operated by Aircloak. This policy describes how the website aircloak.com collects and shares data including personal information. In the context of this policy, “personal information” means information that identifies you such as your name, address, email address or similar contact information. By continuing to use aircloak.com, you consent that you agree to the terms of this policy.

Aircloak may monitor and analyze the performance of aircloak.com.

We collect some basic data in server logs, like your web request, the data sent in response to that request, the browser type, the browser language, the Internet Protocol address and a timestamp for the request. All this information is used to maintain the functionality of aircloak.com. We do not collect personal information automatically for monitoring or performance analysis purposes on aircloak.com.

On aircloak.com, you may be invited to participate in sign-up based features.

Newsletters and similar features may require you to provide personal information in order to participate. Our mailing list is operated by MailChimp and therefore your e-mail address is saved on MailChimp servers. MailChimp is a service used by many corporations. You may view MailChimp's privacy policy regarding the distribution list they maintain on our behalf at mailchimp.com/legal/privacy. Your decision to utilize any sign-up based features and provide such data will always be voluntary. Whenever we ask for this information, we will tell you how we’ll use it or why we’re asking for it.

Aircloak takes reasonable steps to secure data.

We have systems, policies, and procedures in place to provide reasonable protection against unauthorized access, use, or alteration of our collected data including personal information.

Aircloak does not share personal information you have provided to us without your consent, unless

- doing so is legally required
- to otherwise protect our property, legal rights, or that of others.

If Aircloak should ever file for bankruptcy or have its assets sold to or merged with another entity, information Aircloak receives from you from this website is an Aircloak asset and may be transferred.

Aircloak is committed to being as transparent as possible about our privacy practices.

Being a young website, aircloak.com is expected to undergo several changes in the foreseeable future. Any of those changes that lessens the restrictions we place on the use of your personal information will lead to a change in this policy. We will update the effective date at the top of the policy.

If you have any questions, comments, or suggestions regarding this policy, please use the address found in the legal notes, or you can reach the Aircloak team at info@aircloak.com.
