Cryptographic Proof and
Oversight with Teeth

Handling personal data correctly is as hard as it is important. At Aircloak, we strongly believe that by making what we do absolutely open and clear to everybody, we can not only create an environment of trust, but also use the wisdom of the crowd to keep improving user privacy.

Using specialized cryptographic hardware, we make use of a technology called "remote attestation". This effectively enables any interested party to inspect our installed critical code line by line. Certain auditors are furthermore given a real, technological – not only political – power to approve what we do. If they don't, our Cloak cannot decrypt user data any more.

That is why we call it "Oversight with Teeth".
